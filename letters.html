"Tumhari muskurahat... sabse khaas paighaam hoti hai."

Dear you,

I don’t even know where to begin. There’s so much I’ve always wanted to say but never had the words — until now. Maybe because some feelings aren’t meant to be said out loud... they’re meant to be written.

This is one of those letters, tucked away inside a dream and sent only to the one it was meant for. You.

Forever yours,

Someone who's always been watching you smile.
