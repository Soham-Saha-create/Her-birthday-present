Hey Chandu,

Crazy how it’s been such a long time since we first met, first talked, and first became friends. So much has happened in between, hasn’t it? I’ve had my share of mess-ups, and we’ve had our share of misunderstandings, arguments, and fights.

But something has always remained constant in my life — it’s you. You’ve been the Northern Star in my sky, never shifting from the place you hold in my heart. I know I’ve broken your trust, shattered it more than once. Maybe all you needed was time, and I failed to be patient enough to give it to you.

But after everything, something really clicked inside me. I’ve tried to run from it, bury it, distract myself. But where has my heart always settled? With you. It’s always been you.

Even after everything, you were there when I needed someone the most. During those sleepless nights, when I was crying my heart out, you listened — without judgment, without frustration. Even if it wasn’t easy, you stayed. And oh, how I love you for that.

I didn’t write this letter as a proposal — it’s not. But I can say this much: I’m trying to be better. Not for anyone else, not even for you... but so I’m worthy if you ever do choose me.

Yours Loving,
Soham
