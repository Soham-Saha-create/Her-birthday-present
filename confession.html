Hey Chandu,

Crazy how it’s been such a long time since we first met, first talked, first became friends. So much has happened in between, hasn’t it? I’ve had my share of mess-ups. We’ve had our share of misunderstandings, arguments, and fights. But...

Something has always remained constant in my life — it’s you. You’ve been the Northern Star in my sky, never shifting from the place you hold in my heart. I know I’ve broken your trust, shattered it multiple times. Maybe all you needed was time, and I failed to be patient enough.

But after everything, something really clicked inside me. I’ve tried everything, but where has my heart always settled? On you. It’s always been you.

Even after everything, you were there when I needed someone the most. During those sleepless nights, when I was crying my heart out, you were the one who listened — without judgment, without frustration. Even if you didn’t want to, you never said a bad word. And oh, do I love you for that!

I didn’t write this letter as a proposal — it’s not. But I can say this much: I’m trying to be better. Not so you choose me, but so I’m worthy if you ever do.

Yours Loving,
Soham
